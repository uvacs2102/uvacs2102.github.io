Syllabus PDF for Printing cs2102: Discrete Mathematics Mathematics for Computer Science University of Virginia, Fall 2016 Meetings: Tuesdays and Thursdays, 2:00-3:15pm in Chemistry 402. Course Objective. The goal of this course is to students to understand, explain, and apply the foundational mathematical concepts at the core of computer science. The skills students learn in this course are essential for later courses (especially cs3102 and cs4102, but also in other courses), where they will be used to reason about and develop algorithms, prove properties about programs, and understand the limits of different models of computation.
